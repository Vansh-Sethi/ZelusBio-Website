Our Values.

There's a lot of things you can't buy - values and company culture are some of them. At Zelus, we believe that those two factors alone can make or break a vision, so we care about them. Whether you're looking to join our team, or whether you're just looking around, keep in mind that this is the framework we've used to make every decision we've ever made, and we hope to keep it that way. Whenever trying to solve tough challenges, it's important to have some principles you can follow regardless of what happens. Here are what we like to call our four commandments:

NUMBER ONE

Always Try Surpassing Your Limits.

Using automated web-scraping systems, Zelus creates a comprehensive dataset of millions of vaccine candidate compounds and their molecular properties.

NUMBER TWO

Be Relentless For Better

With cutting-edge machine learning (ML) models designed to find complex features in complex data, compounds are narrowed and tested down to a single candidate.

NUMBER THREE

Be Relentless For Better

Zelus engineers the future by developing and releasing the first ever effective cancer prevention vaccine - all brought to life in under 5 years.

NUMBER FOUR

Be Humble.

By validating our process with our MVP, we will be able to conduct more research with ML technologies to find the universal vaccine.
